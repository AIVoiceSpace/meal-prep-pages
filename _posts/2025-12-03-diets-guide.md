## Introduction

Embarking on a new diet can be challenging. But what's even more challenging is planning, preparing, and storing meals that align with your dietary needs. Whether you're on a ketogenic (keto) diet, a palaeolithic (paleo) diet, or any other diet, meal prep is a game-changer. In this guide, you'll learn how to master meal prep for different diets, save time, money, and enjoy delicious meals every day.

## The Challenge

One of the main challenges of following a specific diet is maintaining consistency. It's all too easy to fall off the wagon when you're short on time or lack the energy to cook. Plus, figuring out what foods align with your diet and ensuring you're getting the right nutrients can be confusing. As one r/MealPrepSunday member noted, "Finding keto-friendly meals that I can prepare in advance and actually enjoy has been a struggle."

## Sunday Meal Prep Flow

The key to successful meal prep is having a solid workflow. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you stay on track:

### Planning
Start by planning your meals for the week. Research diet-specific recipes, make a list of ingredients you'll need, and create a shopping list. 

### Prep
Once you have all your ingredients, it's time to start prepping. Clean, chop, and portion out your ingredients to make the cooking process smoother. 

### Cook
Next, cook your meals following your recipes. Make sure to have your [INSTANT_POT_LINK] or [SLOW_COOKER_LINK] handy for easy and efficient cooking.

### Store
Finally, store your meals in [GLASS_CONTAINERS_LINK], ensuring they're portioned for each day of the week. 

Remember, each phase may take some time, so be patient and enjoy the process!
